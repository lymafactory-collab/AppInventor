Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our mobile application ("the App") handles information when users access and use the application.

The App provides access to an online web platform through an embedded browser interface. The App itself does not directly collect personal information from users.

2. Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or process personal information directly.

However, when using the App, users may interact with external web services and websites that may collect information according to their own privacy policies. Such information may include:

  • Account information entered by users
  • Usage data
  • Cookies or session identifiers
  • Device information

This information is processed by the external website accessed through the App.

3. WebView Content

The App loads content from a third-party website using a secure web connection. Any data entered within the website is handled by that website and not by the App itself.

4. Cookies and Tracking

The website displayed in the App may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to improve user experience, maintain sessions, and analyze usage.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party services provided by the website loaded within the application. These services may have their own privacy policies governing how they use collected information.

6. Data Security

The App does not store user data locally except for temporary caching required to display website content.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
